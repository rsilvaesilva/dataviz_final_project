# Data Visualization Project 01

For this project I found data about the American belief system when it pertains to believing in God, Angels, Heaven, Hell and The Devil. In this project I go over many factors that seem to affect how likely one is to believe in these things, If they are rich or poor, Protestant or Catholic or Atheist, their political affeliation, age and gender, and their education level. And indeed these things cause variance in how likely one is to believe in all 5 of those things with things that have shocked me while others I expected it.
